Welcome!

The Cat Sanctuary has been functional since 2012, where as couple of volunteers took care of stranded cats on the streets. The community build a shelter for the cats and the idea for the Sanctuary was born. The website is helping the abbandoned kittens to get a new home. The Cat Sanctuary premises are situated in Helsinki,Finland. Check the cats that are looking for a new home!Feel free to look around. Any donations are also appreciated on the link below.
